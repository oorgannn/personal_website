About Me

My digital journey began at 14, navigating the chaotic early days of Instagram content creation long before Reels existed. What started as a teenager's curiosity about questionable memes evolved into a deeper passion for human psychology.

Today, as a Communication Science graduate, I always try to take a look beyond metrics. The key for a successful project is the people behind it, the meaning of it all.

I really like to explore outside my comfort zone. From managing cross-cultural Discord communities to experimenting with AI-driven production pipelines in Python, I constantly explore the intersection of technology and human connection. Whether I'm editing a complex documentary in Premiere Pro or coding API calls for content automation, my work is driven by a single question: Why?

The deeper I look into myself, the more I can see and feel the people around me.
